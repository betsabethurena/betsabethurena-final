About GreenTech Solutions

Our story, mission, and journey toward a sustainable future.

Our Story

GreenTech Solutions began in 2020 as a collaborative college project at New Mexico State University. A group of engineering students joined forces with the Environmental Science department to explore how technology could be used to reduce environmental impact.

What started as a classroom idea quickly evolved into a real startup focused on developing eco-friendly technologies that help businesses reduce their carbon footprint and operate more sustainably.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainability through innovative technology. We aim to educate, empower, and equip organizations with tools like energy-efficient systems, smart recycling solutions, and carbon tracking software.

Milestones
